## What to backup from work computer

This is a list of important files and directories that should be backed up by an automatic tool.

* `~/.ssh`
* `~/.bashrc`, `~/.bash_profile` and `~/.bash_history`
* `~/.viminfo`, `~/.vimrc` files and `~/.vim/` dir
* `~/.config/` dir
* `~/.gnupg/` dir
* `~/.gitconfig`
* `~/.profile`
* `~/.kube/` dir
* `~/.aws/` dir
* `~/.mozilla/firefox/` dir
* `~/Documents/` dir
